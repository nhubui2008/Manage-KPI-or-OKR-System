### Slide 9 - Workflow Nghiep Vu End-To-End

**Nguoi trinh bay:** Luong he thong

**Thoi luong:** 1.5 phut

**Noi dung tren slide:**

```text
Mission/Vision
    -> OKR
    -> Key Result
    -> KPI
    -> Phan bo KPI cho phong ban / nhan vien
    -> Nhan vien check-in tien do
    -> Manager/Director/HR/Admin duyet check-in
    -> Cap nhat tien do KPI
    -> Tinh total score theo trong so
    -> Map sang Grading Rank
    -> Tinh Expected Bonus
    -> Tao/Cap nhat Evaluation Result
    -> Gui Director review
    -> Dashboard / Reports / AI Insights
```

- Neu check-in bi tu choi: khong tinh vao diem chinh thuc
- Neu check-in duoc duyet: he thong cap nhat diem, rank va thuong du kien
- Evaluation co vong `PendingDirectorReview` de Director chot ket qua cuoi

**Goi y loi noi:**

> Luong nghiep vu bat dau tu Mission/Vision, sau do chuyen thanh OKR va Key Result. Tu Key Result, doanh nghiep co the tao KPI theo ky danh gia va phan bo cho phong ban hoac nhan vien. Khi nhan vien check-in, du lieu khong duoc tinh ngay ma di qua buoc review. Sau khi duoc duyet, he thong moi tinh tien do, tong diem, xep hang va thuong du kien, sau do day vao evaluation va bao cao.

### Slide 15 - Kien Truc Ky Thuat, ERD Va Backend

**Nguoi trinh bay:** Backend

**Thoi luong:** 2.75 phut

**Noi dung tren slide:**

- Tech stack:
  - ASP.NET Core MVC, .NET 10
  - Entity Framework Core 10
  - SQL Server
  - Cookie Authentication, Google OAuth
  - EPPlus export Excel
  - Gemini API cho AI
- Kien truc request:
  - Browser -> Middleware -> Controller -> Service/Helper -> MiniERPDbContext -> SQL Server
  - Razor Views render ket qua ve browser
- Cac nhom bang ERD:
  - Foundation: Roles, Permissions, Role_Permissions, SystemUsers, Employees, Departments, Positions
  - OKR: MissionVisions, OKRs, OKRKeyResults, OKR_Mission_Mappings, OKR_Department_Allocations, OKR_Employee_Allocations
  - KPI: EvaluationPeriods, KPITypes, KPIProperties, KPIs, KPIDetails, KPI_Department_Assignments, KPI_Employee_Assignments
  - Check-in: KPICheckIns, CheckInDetails, CheckInStatuses, CheckInHistoryLogs, GoalComments
  - Evaluation/HR: EvaluationResults, GradingRanks, BonusRules, RealtimeExpectedBonuses, HRExportReports
  - System/AI: SystemAlerts, AuditLogs, AIGenerationHistories
- Phan quyen:
  - `[Authorize]` yeu cau dang nhap
  - `[HasPermission("PERMISSION_CODE")]` chan thao tac theo permission
  - Claim role va PermissionClaimsTransformation bo sung quyen
- Service:
  - GeminiService: goi Gemini API
  - AIDataService: gom ngu canh KPI/OKR theo quyen
  - AIAlertService: tao smart alerts
  - NotificationService: thong bao
  - OKRProgressService/ProgressHelper: tinh tien do
  - EmailService: OTP va email
- Bao mat va cau hinh:
  - Cookie HttpOnly, SameSite, SecurePolicy theo environment
  - Antiforgery token cho form
  - Data Protection keys de cookie song qua recycle IIS
  - Environment config qua `.env` va appsettings

**Goi y loi noi:**

> Ve backend, he thong dung ASP.NET Core MVC nen request di tu browser qua middleware, vao controller, sau do controller goi service hoac DbContext. EF Core lam viec voi SQL Server, con Razor Views render giao dien. Database duoc chia theo nhom nghiep vu: nen tang to chuc, OKR, KPI, check-in, evaluation, bao cao va AI. Diem quan trong la phan quyen khong chi o giao dien ma nam ca o controller thong qua Authorize va HasPermission. Vi vay ngay ca khi nguoi dung goi truc tiep URL, he thong van kiem tra quyen truoc khi xu ly.

### 4.1. PO - Gioi Thieu Bai Toan, Khach Hang, Muc Tieu Va Pham Vi

**Thoi luong:** 3 phut

**Noi dung can trinh bay:**

> Phan dau tien, em xin trinh bay goc nhin san pham. Du an cua nhom la VietMach KPI/OKR System, mot he thong web giup doanh nghiep quan ly KPI va OKR theo mot quy trinh lien tuc.
>
> Qua viec phan tich nhu cau nguoi dung, nhom nhan thay nhieu doanh nghiep van quan ly KPI bang Excel, file rieng le hoac trao doi thu cong. Cach lam nay co ba van de: thu nhat, muc tieu chien luoc va KPI hang ngay khong duoc ket noi ro; thu hai, nhan vien check-in tien do nhung quan ly kho theo doi trang thai; thu ba, ket qua danh gia cuoi ky de thieu minh bach vi khong co lich su review day du.
>
> Vi vay, nhom de xuat mot he thong tap trung, trong do doanh nghiep co the tao Mission/Vision, tao OKR, chia nho thanh Key Result, sau do gan KPI cho phong ban hoac nhan vien. Nhan vien se check-in tien do, quan ly duyet ket qua, he thong tinh diem, xep hang, thuong du kien va tao bao cao.
>
> Doi tuong su dung gom Admin, Director, Manager, HR va Employee/Sales. Moi vai tro co pham vi quyen rieng. Vi du Employee chi xem KPI cua minh, Manager quan ly KPI phong ban, Director nhin toan canh va duyet ket qua cuoi, HR quan ly nhan su va bao cao.
>
> Pham vi chinh cua du an gom quan ly OKR, KPI, check-in, evaluation, dashboard, bao cao Excel va AI Insights. Ngoai ra co cac chuc nang nen tang nhu tai khoan, role, permission, nhan vien, phong ban, chuc vu, audit log, quen mat khau qua email va Google OAuth.

**Cau chuyen tiep sang SM:**

> Sau khi xac dinh bai toan va pham vi san pham, nhom can mot cach quan ly cong viec de dam bao cac module duoc phat trien dung tien do. Phan tiep theo se do vai tro Scrum Master trinh bay.

---

### 4.2. SM - Quy Trinh Lam Viec, Backlog Va Definition Of Done

**Thoi luong:** 3 phut

**Noi dung can trinh bay:**

> Voi vai tro Scrum Master, em trinh bay cach nhom to chuc cong viec. Vi he thong co nhieu module va moi module lai lien quan den nhau, nhom ap dung Scrum de chia nho thanh cac sprint.
>
> Product backlog duoc xay dung tu cac user story. Vi du: voi Employee, can story "nhan vien co the xem KPI duoc giao va gui check-in"; voi Manager, can story "quan ly co the duyet check-in cua nhan vien trong phong ban"; voi Director, can story "giam doc co the xem dashboard toan cong ty va duyet ket qua evaluation".
>
> Khi vao sprint backlog, nhom chia story thanh task cu the cho frontend, backend va tester. Backend xu ly controller, service, database va phan quyen. Frontend xu ly giao dien Razor, bang, form, dashboard. Tester viet test case theo luong nghiep vu va role.
>
> Nhom theo doi tien do bang Trello voi cac cot Backlog, To Do, In Progress, Review, Testing va Done. Git duoc dung de quan ly ma nguon, commit theo tinh nang va kiem tra thay doi truoc khi hop nhat.
>
> Definition of Done cua nhom gom: tinh nang chay duoc, dung yeu cau, dung phan quyen, co validation, giao dien khong vo layout, tester da kiem tra test case chinh, va khong lam hong cac luong da co. Dac biet, voi he thong nay, phan quyen la tieu chi bat buoc vi moi vai tro co pham vi du lieu khac nhau.

**Cau chuyen tiep sang Luong he thong:**

> Sau khi nhom da quan ly duoc quy trinh phat trien, phan tiep theo se di vao luong he thong de thay cac module lien ket voi nhau nhu the nao.

---

### 4.3. Luong He Thong - Use Case, Workflow, UI Flow Va Phan Ra Chuc Nang

**Thoi luong:** 4 phut

**Noi dung can trinh bay:**

> Em se trinh bay luong he thong. He thong co cac tac nhan chinh la Admin, Director, Manager, HR va Employee/Sales. Admin quan tri he thong, role, permission va danh muc. Director quan ly muc tieu chien luoc, xem dashboard toan cong ty va duyet evaluation cuoi. Manager tao va phan bo KPI, duyet check-in va gui evaluation len Director. HR quan ly nhan su, ky danh gia va bao cao. Employee/Sales xem KPI duoc giao va check-in tien do.
>
> Luong nghiep vu end-to-end bat dau tu Mission/Vision. Tu muc tieu chien luoc nay, he thong tao OKR va Key Result. Sau do KPI duoc tao theo ky danh gia va co the lien ket voi OKR hoac Key Result. KPI sau khi duoc duyet se duoc phan bo cho phong ban hoac nhan vien kem trong so.
>
> Khi nhan vien check-in, ban ghi mac dinh o trang thai Pending. Manager, Director, HR hoac Admin se review. Neu bi Rejected, check-in khong duoc tinh vao diem chinh thuc. Neu Approved, he thong cap nhat tien do KPI, tinh total score theo trong so, map sang GradingRank, tinh Expected Bonus va tao hoac cap nhat EvaluationResult.
>
> EvaluationResult sau do co the duoc Manager gui len Director review. Director se duyet hoac tu choi. Khi duoc duyet, ket qua cuoi ky co the hien thi tren dashboard va bao cao.
>
> Ve UI flow, Director thuong vao Dashboard, OKRs va EvaluationReports. Manager vao KPIs, AllocatePersonnel, ReviewQueue va EvaluationResults. Employee vao Dashboard ca nhan, KPIs va KPICheckIns/Create. HR vao Employees, EvaluationPeriods, BonusRules va EvaluationReports.
>
> Neu phan ra chuc nang, he thong gom cac nhom: nen tang to chuc, chien luoc OKR, KPI, thuc thi check-in, danh gia nhan su, bao cao va AI. Neu phan tang ky thuat, he thong gom Presentation layer, Controller layer, Service/Helper layer, Data Access layer, Database layer va External Services.

**Cau chuyen tiep sang Frontend:**

> Khi cac luong nghiep vu da ro, frontend se la phan giup nguoi dung thao tac voi cac luong do mot cach truc quan va dung vai tro.

---

### 4.4. Frontend - Thiet Ke Giao Dien Va Trai Nghiem Nguoi Dung

**Thoi luong:** 3 phut

**Noi dung can trinh bay:**

> Ve frontend, nhom xay dung giao dien theo phong cach enterprise dashboard. Ly do la he thong phuc vu quan ly noi bo, nen giao dien can ro rang, de doc va ho tro xem so lieu nhanh.
>
> Mau sac chu dao la xanh duong chuyen nghiep, nen sang, sidebar xanh dam. Sidebar giup dieu huong giua cac module nhu Dashboard, OKR, KPI, Check-in, Evaluation, Employees va Reports. Diem quan trong la sidebar khong hien thi giong nhau cho moi nguoi, ma an/hien theo permission.
>
> Cac man hinh chinh gom Dashboard, OKRs, KPIs, KPICheckIns, ReviewQueue, EvaluationResults va EvaluationReports. Dashboard dung card va bieu do de hien thi KPI/OKR/check-in theo ky danh gia. OKR va KPI dung bang du lieu va form de tao, sua, duyet, phan bo. Check-in co form nhap gia tri thuc dat va ghi chu. ReviewQueue giup quan ly duyet cac check-in dang Pending.
>
> Ve trai nghiem nguoi dung, nhom chu trong cac trang thai nghiep vu nhu Pending, Approved, Rejected, Draft va PendingDirectorReview. Cac trang thai nay can hien thi ro de nguoi dung biet du lieu dang o buoc nao. He thong cung co alert, notification, validation form va trang AccessDenied khi nguoi dung khong co quyen.
>
> Frontend su dung Razor Views ket hop Bootstrap, jQuery, Select2, Chart.js va SweetAlert2. Cach ket hop nay giup giao dien du on dinh, de phat trien nhanh va phu hop voi mo hinh ASP.NET Core MVC.

**Cau chuyen tiep sang Backend:**

> Phia sau cac man hinh nay la backend xu ly nghiep vu, database, phan quyen va tich hop AI. Phan tiep theo se trinh bay kien truc ky thuat cua he thong.

---

### 4.5. Backend - Kien Truc Ky Thuat, ERD, Phan Quyen Va AI

**Thoi luong:** 4 phut

**Noi dung can trinh bay:**

> Ve backend, he thong duoc xay dung bang ASP.NET Core MVC tren .NET 10, dung Entity Framework Core 10 de lam viec voi SQL Server. He thong co cookie authentication, Google OAuth, export Excel bang EPPlus, va tich hop Gemini API cho cac tinh nang AI.
>
> Luong request co the hieu nhu sau: nguoi dung thao tac tren browser, request di qua middleware nhu HTTPS, routing, authentication, authorization va antiforgery. Sau do request vao Controller. Controller se goi service, helper hoac MiniERPDbContext. EF Core truy van SQL Server va Razor Views render ket qua tra ve giao dien.
>
> Ve ERD, database duoc chia thanh nhieu nhom bang. Nhom foundation gom Roles, Permissions, Role_Permissions, SystemUsers, Employees, Departments va Positions. Nhom OKR gom MissionVisions, OKRs, OKRKeyResults va cac bang allocation. Nhom KPI gom EvaluationPeriods, KPITypes, KPIProperties, KPIs, KPIDetails va KPI assignments. Nhom check-in gom KPICheckIns, CheckInDetails, CheckInStatuses, CheckInHistoryLogs va GoalComments. Nhom evaluation gom EvaluationResults, GradingRanks, BonusRules va RealtimeExpectedBonuses. Ngoai ra co AuditLogs, SystemAlerts va AIGenerationHistories.
>
> Phan quyen la diem quan trong cua backend. He thong dung `[Authorize]` de yeu cau dang nhap va `[HasPermission("PERMISSION_CODE")]` de chan thao tac theo permission. Permission duoc gan qua Role_Permission. Vi vay, khong chi giao dien an menu, ma backend cung chan truc tiep neu nguoi dung khong co quyen.
>
> Cac service chinh gom GeminiService de goi AI, AIDataService de gom ngu canh KPI/OKR theo quyen, AIAlertService de tao smart alert, NotificationService de thong bao, EmailService de gui OTP/email, va OKRProgressService de tinh tien do OKR. AI chi la lop ho tro phan tich va goi y, khong thay the logic nghiep vu loi.
>
> Ve bao mat va cau hinh, he thong dung cookie HttpOnly, SameSite, SecurePolicy theo environment, antiforgery token cho form, Data Protection keys de cookie khong bi mat khi IIS recycle, va cau hinh qua `.env`, appsettings va environment variables.

**Cau chuyen tiep sang Tester:**

> Khi he thong co nhieu luong nghiep vu, nhieu role va nhieu tinh toan, kiem thu la phan rat quan trong de dam bao san pham dang tin cay.

---

### 4.6. Tester - Test Plan, Test Case Va Dam Bao Chat Luong

**Thoi luong:** 3 phut

**Noi dung can trinh bay:**

> Ve kiem thu, muc tieu cua nhom la dam bao he thong dung nghiep vu KPI/OKR, dung phan quyen, dung tinh toan va on dinh ve giao dien.
>
> Test plan duoc chia thanh nhieu nhom. Functional test kiem tra tung module nhu Auth, Employees, OKRs, KPIs, KPICheckIns, EvaluationResults va Reports. Role/permission test kiem tra moi vai tro chi duoc thay va thao tac dung pham vi. Workflow test kiem tra luong end-to-end tu Mission/Vision den OKR, KPI, check-in, review, evaluation va report. UI/UX test kiem tra form, bang, sidebar, alert va responsive. Report test kiem tra export Excel. AI test kiem tra chat, goi y va fallback khi thieu API key hoac Gemini loi.
>
> Mot so test case quan trong gom: Employee dang nhap chi thay KPI duoc giao; Employee tao check-in thi trang thai la Pending; Manager duyet check-in cua nhan vien trong phong ban; Manager khong duoc duyet check-in cua chinh minh; Check-in Approved phai cap nhat score, rank va expected bonus; Director duyet EvaluationResult o trang thai PendingDirectorReview; role khong co permission bi chuyen den AccessDenied; export Excel tao duoc bao cao dung ky danh gia.
>
> Definition of Done cua tester la test case chinh pass, khong co loi nghiem trong tren workflow, khong co loi phan quyen, va cac loi giao dien chinh da duoc sua. Vi he thong lien quan den danh gia nhan su, tester uu tien cac loi co the lam sai ket qua cuoi ky hoac lo du lieu khong dung pham vi.
>
> Ket luan, VietMach KPI/OKR System quan ly tron vong doi tu chien luoc den thuc thi, phe duyet, danh gia va bao cao. He thong giup du lieu KPI/OKR tap trung, minh bach, co phan quyen va co AI ho tro phan tich.

## 5. Goi Y So Do De Dua Vao PowerPoint

### 5.1. So Do Workflow

```text
Mission/Vision
  -> OKR
  -> Key Result
  -> KPI
  -> KPI Assignment
  -> KPI Check-in
  -> Review
  -> Score/Rank/Bonus
  -> Evaluation Result
  -> Director Review
  -> Dashboard/Report/AI
```

### 5.2. So Do Kien Truc

```text
Browser
  -> ASP.NET Core Middleware
  -> Controllers
  -> Services / Helpers
  -> MiniERPDbContext
  -> SQL Server

External:
  -> Gemini API
  -> SMTP Email
```

### 5.3. So Do ERD Rut Gon

```text
Role --< SystemUser -- Employee >-- Department
Role --< Role_Permission >-- Permission

MissionVision --< OKR --< OKRKeyResult
OKR --< KPI --< KPIDetail
KPI --< KPI_Employee_Assignment >-- Employee
KPI --< KPICheckIn >-- Employee
KPICheckIn --< CheckInDetail

Employee --< EvaluationResult >-- EvaluationPeriod
EvaluationResult -- GradingRank
GradingRank --< BonusRule
Employee --< RealtimeExpectedBonus
```

```text
Presentation Layer:
  Razor Views, Bootstrap, jQuery, CSS, Chart.js

Controller Layer:
  Auth, Dashboard, OKRs, KPIs, KPICheckIns, EvaluationResults, AI

Service/Helper Layer:
  GeminiService, AIDataService, EmailService, NotificationService, ProgressHelper

Data Access Layer:
  Entity Framework Core, MiniERPDbContext

Database Layer:
  SQL Server

External Services:
  Gemini API, SMTP Email, Google OAuth
```
